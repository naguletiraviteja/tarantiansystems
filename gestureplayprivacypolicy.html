Privacy Policy for GesturePlay

Effective Date: 25-08-2025

Developer: Tarantian Systems LLP

1. Introduction

GesturePlay (“we”, “our”, “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use our mobile application GesturePlay.

2. Information We Collect

a) Camera Access: We use your device’s camera to detect hand gestures and body posture for gameplay. All processing happens locally on your device. We do not capture, store, or transmit any photos or videos.

b) Device Information: We may collect non-personal information such as device model, OS version, app version, crash reports, and analytics (via Google Play Services/Firebase).

c) Advertising Data (AdMob): We use Google AdMob to show ads. AdMob may collect Advertising ID, IP address, device information, and app usage statistics. For details, see Google Privacy & Terms.

3. How We Use Information

  • To enable gesture and posture gameplay.
  • To improve app performance through analytics.
  • To show advertisements through AdMob.
  • To ensure app stability and security.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data. Ads and analytics are handled by Google AdMob and related services. We may disclose information if required by law.

5. Children’s Privacy

The app is suitable for general audiences, including children. However, ads may still appear. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

6. Data Security

Gesture recognition runs locally on your device. No gameplay images or videos are uploaded. Analytics and ads are handled by trusted providers like Google.

7. Your Choices

  • You can disable camera access in device settings (this will disable gameplay).
  • You can manage ad personalization in your Google account settings.
  • You can uninstall the app to stop all data collection.

8. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted in the app and/or on our official website.
